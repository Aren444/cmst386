How Blissful Bakes Works

Whether you need a simple tart for a quiet night in or a fully custom cake for a milestone celebration, this page explains what to expect from first idea to final pickup or delivery.

1. Browse the menu and gallery

Start by exploring the Menu and Gallery pages to get a sense of flavors, styles, and portion sizes. You can mix standard items like tarts and cookies with custom cakes for larger events right inside your cart!

2. Use the Cake Builder for custom ideas

If you would like a custom cake, visit the Cake Builder page to choose sizes, flavors, fillings, and frosting styles. Then, add it right to your cart!

3. Share details on the contact form

When you are ready, go to the Contact page and tell me about your event, guest count, date, and any allergies or design notes. You can share as much or as little detail as you like.

4. Confirm pricing and availability

After I receive your message, I will follow up by email to confirm availability, final design, and pricing within 24 hours. For larger or more detailed orders, we may adjust flavors or decorations to fit your budget.

5. Pickup or local delivery

On the day of your order, you can pick up at the agreed time or arrange local delivery. Desserts are packed with care instructions so they arrive beautifully.

Timing and lead times

More than five days notice is ideal for most items. For custom cakes or larger events, a minimum of two weeks notice usually gives enough time to plan designs, test colors, confirm ingredients, etc.

If you have a last minute request, you are still welcome to ask. Small orders can possibly get squeezed in and sometimes there is a cancellation that frees up time.

Allergies and special requests

Blissful Bakes offers some gluten friendly and nut conscious options, but the kitchen is not a certified allergen free space. Please use the contact form to list any allergies or strong dislikes so I can suggest safe, thoughtful dessert choices for your group.
