Store Policies & Important Information

Allergen Information

While care is taken to avoid cross contamination, Blissful Bakes regularly uses flour, eggs, dairy, and nuts in many recipes. Please inform us of any allergies before placing an order so we can take appropriate steps to help you choose safe options.

Delivery & Pickup

Local deliveries are available within 10 miles of Covington, WA. Pickup orders may be scheduled with 48 hours notice. Seasonal weather or high demand may affect scheduling, but we do our best to meet your preferred date.

Refund Policy

Orders canceled within 48 hours are fully refundable. Custom cakes require additional time so 50% of the deposit (paid for by adding your cake in the cake-builder) will be refunded if cancelled within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery/pickup time.

Privacy Policy

Blissful Bakes respects your privacy. Information submitted through the contact form, (name, email, home address) is only used to respond to your inquiry or prepare your order. I do not sell or share personal information with third parties. Cookies might be used to improve the site's performance, but no tracking or advertising tools are used on this site. If you wish to update or remove your contact information after submitting a form, contact me directly to make that request.
